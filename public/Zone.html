Zone Valve

The Siral Type-Z zone valves are designed for easy and efficient installation, adjustability and operation. The Type Z zone valve offers easy field adjustable Cv value, normal position and fail position changes.

Compact and robust zone valves

  • Field adjustable Cv and setup for current and changing design requirements.
  • Zero leakage eliminating energy waste.
  • Over 90% less power consumption than conventional zone valves.

Common Applications

for Zone Valves

  • Fan Coil Units
  • Unit Ventilators
  • Radiant Panels
