Common Applications

The Siral Z series Zone Valve is a control valve ideal for use in fan coils, unit ventilators, heating and cooling coils and VAV re-heat coils as a change over or bypass sequence is required.. The Z series Zone Valve efficiently helps controls the flow of media for heating and cooling coils. The Z series Zone Valve available in both fail-safe and non fail-safe applications.

Operations

The Siral Z series Zone Valve is an electronically actuated control ball valve. Control signal options include on/off, floating point or modulating (proportional) and are used to determine the final positioning of the ball rotation angle which determines the amount of flow through the valve.
